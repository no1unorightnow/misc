1. Tiles - where to get tiles
	1. Generators vs STLs
1. Mounts - how are things attaching?
	1. Direct vs Multiconnect; Snaps vs threads, etc.
1. Object holders
	1. cables, objects, etc.
		1. STLs vs generators
