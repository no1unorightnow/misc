## HartSmart Products Filament Recycling

- Bins fop PLA, PETG, TPU
- Even the **TINIEST** scrap of the wrong type can ruin an entire batch - please thow away elsewhere if you can't guarantee type purity
- PLA additives/variations:
	- OKAY to recycle:
		- Silks
		- Non-glitter additves (such as Polymaker's "Metallic PLA" and "Starlight PLA" lines)
		- Matte
		- Pro
		- Plus/+
  - NOT okay:
	- Carbon fiber
    - Glass fiber
    - Glitter (such as Polymaker's Galaxy line)
    - Wood
    - Metal
    - Glow/Glow-in-the-dark/phosphors
